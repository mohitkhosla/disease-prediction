About Us!


Symptoms Based Disease Production, Inc. is a private not-for-profit corporation that is proud to offer quality health care to the residents of Mumbai, India. This organization oversees the operations of predicting the diseases based on the symptoms entered by the user, making it a free health agency. The organization also oversees the operations of multiple diseases and provides us with the comparison amongst the same. The residents and patients are served by the facility and agency.


The Management team of our system, are dedicated to maintaining a high level of patient service through a continuum of care that includes long and short-term in-patient symptoms and medication care as well as in-home services on-the-go. The Doctor's team is made up of volunteer members of the community that have experience in various backgrounds including healthcare, legal, engineering, financial and education.


Their experience along with the extensive health care and financial experience provided by the Doctor's team has allowed the system to continue to grow and provide quality, progressive health care. Please visit the clinics of respective Doctors' for personalised routine checkups:

  • One To One Moderation and Help
  • All About Home Care Remedies
